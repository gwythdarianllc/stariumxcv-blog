**What's a niche interest or fandom you'll gladly go on too long about?**

Breakcore. I think because of ADHD I have a tendency to like and listen to a song just for one small specific part, like a riff or drum break, and breakcore is just a series of complex and interesting drum breaks. The genre grew out of Jungle and early Drum n Bass. Alec Empire (also lead of the band Atari Teenage Riot) is credited as being one of the  pioneers of the Breakcore genre with his album “The Destroyer” in 1996. From the 2000s the genre has split into many sub-genres, from many with heavy metal influences, to others that are more melodic and ambient. The most well known artist is likely Aphex Twin, with other notable artists including: Ruby My Dear (my personal favorite), Venetian Snares, Igorrr, Squarepusher… oh I’m doing the thing.
